Specifically, Article 67 outlines the role and responsibilities of the board of directors. A board must be established in each joint stock company, unless otherwise provided by Article 63. ### Indicator 4.4: Lack of copyright framework and exceptions
- **Score:** 0
- **Source Legislation:** Law of the Kyrgyz Republic No. 6 of 14 January 1998 on Copyright and Related Rights (КЫРГЫЗ РЕСПУБЛИКАСЫНЫН МЫЙЗАМЫ 1998-жылдын 14-январы № 6 Автордук жана чектеш укуктар жөнүндө)
- **Evidence/Impact:** The Kyrgyzstan has a copyright regime under the Law No. 6. Articles. 18-25 list the exceptions following fair use or fair dealing model, which include use of a work for educational purposes and by persons with print disabilities, use by libraries, archives and educational establishments, limitations and exceptions regarding accessible format copies, among others.

- Personal use: Article 18 allows for the reproduction of works for personal purposes without consent or remuneration. This aligns with the idea of fair use where an individual can use works for personal, non-commercial purposes without infringing on copyright.
- Quotations and educational use: Article 19 allows quotations, educational use, and the reproduction of works for non-commercial purposes like research, education, or reporting current events. This resembles fair dealing exceptions for education and criticism in jurisdictions like the UK or Canada.
- Libraries and archives: Article 20 permits libraries and archives to reproduce works for preservation and educational purposes, which aligns with the fair use/fair dealing exceptions for non-commercial use or archival purposes.
- Public access works: Article 21 allows for the use of works that are permanently in public places (such as architecture or fine art) for reproduction, broadcasting, or communication, which is akin to fair use in public spaces or locations.
- Judicial and administrative use: Article 23 allows reproduction for judicial and administrative proceedings, which aligns with fair use for legal proceedings or public interest.
- Computer program decompilation: Article 25 permits decompilation of computer programs for interoperability purposes. This is a clear example of fair use as it allows for actions necessary for the development of compatible software without infringing on the original copyright.
- **Timeframe:** Since January 1998, as amended in March 2017
