### Indicator 4.2: Patent enforcement issues: civil and administrative procedures and remedies, provisional and other measures
- **Score:** 0
- **Source Legislation:** Patent Law of the People's Republic of China《中华人民共和国专利法》
- **Evidence/Impact:** Civil and administrative procedures:
Articles 46 and 63 allow parties to appeal administrative decisions related to invalidity and compulsory licensing by filing lawsuits with the courts.
Article 46: Any organization or individual who believes that the grant of a patent does not comply with the provisions of this Law may request the administrative department for patent affairs under the State Council to declare the patent right invalid. The administrative department for patent affairs under the State Council shall promptly examine and decide on the request for invalidation of the patent right and notify the parties concerned. The decision on the invalidation of a patent right shall be registered and announced by the administrative department for patent affairs under the State Council. The decision shall be final and binding.
Article 63: In the event of a dispute over patent infringement, the parties concerned may resolve the dispute through mediation. If mediation fails, or if one of the parties refuses mediation, they may bring a lawsuit to the people's court or request the relevant administrative department for patents to handle the matter.
When the relevant administrative department for patents determines the matter, if a party is dissatisfied with the handling decision, they may bring a lawsuit to the people's court within 15 days from the date of receiving the notification. If no lawsuit is brought within the prescribed time limit, the decision shall come into effect and be enforced by the people's court.

Remedies:
Article 71: When the amount of damages for patent infringement cannot be determined based on the losses suffered by the patent holder, the benefits obtained by the infringer, or a reasonable multiple of the patent license fee, the People's Court may award damages ranging from RMB 30,000 to RMB 5,000,000, depending on factors such as the type of patent, nature, and circumstances of the infringement. Where intentional infringement occurs under severe circumstances, the People's Court may determine compensation up to five times the amount calculated based on the above methods. The amount of damages shall also include the reasonable expenses incurred by the patent holder in stopping the infringement.

Provisional Measures:
Article 69 provides patent enforcement agencies the authority to inspect, seize, and secure evidence during suspected patent infringement or counterfeiting investigations.
