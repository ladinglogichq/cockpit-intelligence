### Indicator 2.2: Specific requirements on source codes, encryption and trade secrets
- **Score:** 0
- **Source Legislation:** Protection of Layout Designs of Integrated Circuits Act B.E. 2543
- **Evidence/Impact:** There is no requirement under the Protection of Layout Designs of Integrated Circuits Act to disclose source code, software, or the design logic behind an integrated circuit in order to participate in public procurement. The Act specifically protects layout designs, also referred to as topographies, which are the physical, three-dimensional arrangements of components and interconnections within an integrated circuit. According to Section 6, only those layout designs that are original and not commonly known in the industry are eligible for protection. This protection applies strictly to the physical configuration, and does not extend to the functional logic or any underlying software.

### Indicator 3.1: Foreign equity limits in sectors relevant to digital trade 
Exception: Not cover foreign equity cap in telecom sector (covered under Pillar 5) and e-commerce sector (covered under Pillar 12)
- **Score:** 0
- **Source Legislation:** Foreign Business Act  B.E.2542 (พระราชบัญญัติการประกอบธุรกิจของคนต่างด้าว พ.ศ. 2542)
- **Evidence/Impact:** The Foreign Business Act (FBA) B.E.2542 regulates foreign investments in Thailand. Section 4 of the Act defines "foreigner" as companies with at least one-half of capital or shares held by foreigners as well as a limited partnership or a registered ordinary partnership with foreigners as the managing partner or the manager. Section 8 prescribes the List of businesses that are not allowed foreigners to operate: 
(1) List 1: Businesses stricto sensu not permissible to foreigners by special reason. 
(2) List 2: Businesses related to national safety or security or having impacts on arts, culture, traditions, customs and folklore handicrafts or natural resources and the environment, unless upon obtaining permission from the Minister with the approval of the Council of Ministers.
